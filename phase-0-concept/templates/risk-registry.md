## Scoring Guide

**Likelihood:** How probable is this risk?
- **Low** — Unlikely given what we know
- **Medium** — Could go either way, depends on external factors
- **High** — More likely than not without intervention

**Impact:** If it happens, how much does it hurt?
- **Low** — Minor delay or workaround needed, no scope change
- **Medium** — Requires rework, scope adjustment, or added cost
- **High** — Blocks a product, kills a revenue stream, or forces a major pivot

**Severity** (Likelihood x Impact):
- **Critical** — Must resolve before proceeding
- **High** — Needs a plan, actively monitor
- **Medium** — Have a plan, check periodically
- **Low** — Acknowledged, revisit if conditions change
